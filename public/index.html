ABOUT US

SSN Institutions, founded by Padma Bhushan Dr. Shiv Nadar, Chairman, HCL Technologies, stand out as a premier centre of higher learning with a mission of pursuing excellence in education and research. The institutions are named after the Chairman’s father, Sri Sivasubramaniya Nadar. With their diverse and dynamic community of students, SSN offers a distinctive combination of some of the finest undergraduate, postgraduate and research programs, accomplished faculty, world-class facilities and a residential campus set on a sprawling 250 acres of sylvan surroundings.
